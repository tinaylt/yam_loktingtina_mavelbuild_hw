Mike Wazowski - The most scary monster!

Mike Wazowski is one of the main characters in Monster Unversity. He is a short, lime green spherical monster with one large green eye, two small horns on his head, and thin arms and legs attached to either side of his torso.

Scott "Squishy" Squibbles

"Squishy" as a college student and "Scary" as a Scarer, is one of the five tritagonists in Monster University. He is a five-eyed jelly-like monster; a 18-year-old college student who doesn't know what he wants to be at Monster University, he is also a member of the Oozma Kappa fraternity.
